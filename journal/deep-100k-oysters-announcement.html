Restoration Update

As time has passed, Maorach Beag has evolved and become a key player in the field of environmental restoration. We support various projects that aim to restore Native European Flat Oyster (Ostrea edulis) reefs.

One of the key projects we support is the Glenmorangie DEEP project. This is a ground breaking environmental restoration project that aims to return 4 million oysters to the Dornoch Firth, Scotland in front of their distillery.

They have recently hit a major milestone after 10 years of hard work. It goes without saying that we are so proud to be the key supply chain supplier to this effort and see our oysters going to work!

To celebrate this achievement they have made a film, in which we feature, well just a wee cameo.

We hope you enjoy this and perhaps it shall inspire in some way. For us it is a good reminder that even with all the bad news around the climate there is hope.
